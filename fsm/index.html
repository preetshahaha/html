IITD —AIA Foundation for Smart Manufacturing (FSM)

Under the Samarth Udyog Mission of Department of Heavy Industry (DHI), Govt. of India, IIT Delhi and Automation Industry Association (AIA) together with Industry sponsors have set up facilities, IITD-AIA Foundation for Smart Manufacturing (FSM) that help, support, and develop Smart Manufacturing concepts for Indian Industry to witness, ideate and try out in their own plants. The collaboration is also aimed at developing a holistic educational curriculum and skill building program through a vibrant incubation and administrative environment. FSM Skills is a holistic platform for providing immersive training experience through Live lectures, Online learning, Live demonstrations, Live Labs and Self paced exercises on remotely accessible actual hardware.

Overview of Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT)

This course will give overview of underpinning technologies that makes IIoT possible, the technical issues required to build an IIoT network, and innovative business models that arise from the IIoT. Protocols, software patterns, and middleware IIoT platforms that enables us to connect or visualize our IIoT network will be discussed. Smart Factory connectivity for IIoT, IIoT Use cases and IIoT deployment framework will be discussed
